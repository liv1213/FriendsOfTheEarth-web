Overview

Climate change and the global energy crisis threaten the lives and livelihoods of billions of people worldwide. Ninety-seven per cent of climate scientists agree that human activities produce greenhouse gases that are heating the planet. The primary sources of greenhouse gases are the burning of fossil fuels for energy, industry and transport, industrial agriculture, and deforestation. There are solutions to the climate crisis but we must act now.
